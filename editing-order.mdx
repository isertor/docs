## Before you begin

This guide will walk you through updating order details, including modifying items, customer information, delivery addresses, and fulfilment type.

Some changes, such as modifying delivery times or fulfilment locations, may require rebooking delivery logistics. Follow all steps carefully to ensure updates are correctly applied to your deliveries.

If you are editing an order from a past date, you must have Manager or above permissions. Set permissions from **Settings > Team members**.

## Changing the Serving Date or Time for a Delivery Order

Use this option to adjust when an order will be served.

1. Select a new date or time in the **Serving Date & Serving Time** fields.**Tip:** This is the time when the food will be served to the diner (e.g., **6:30 pm - 7:00 pm**). For delivery orders, preparation starts **30 minutes earlier**.

2. Tap **Update** in the top-right corner to save changes.

3. A pop-up will appear to rebook delivery logistics. **Select the first option** to update the driver’s booking.

## Updating Customer Information (Name, Phone, Email)

1. In the **Customer Details** section, update:

   * **Customer’s name**

   * **Phone number**

   * **Email address**

2. Tap **Update** in the top-right corner.

3. If you update the email address, tap **Resend Confirmation** to send the order confirmation to the new email.

## Updating the Delivery Address

Use this option when a customer provides a different delivery location.

1. In the **Address** section, enter the new address (including unit number) and tap **Add**.

2. Tap **Update** in the top-right corner to save changes.

3. A pop-up will appear to rebook delivery logistics. **Select the first option** to update the driver’s booking to the new address.

## Changing the Serving Outlet

Use this option when an order needs to be fulfilled from a different outlet.

1. Tap **Update** in the top-right corner to save changes.

2. A pop-up will appear to rebook delivery logistics. **Select the first option** to update the driver’s booking.
