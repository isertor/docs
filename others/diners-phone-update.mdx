  **Who is this article for?**

  * Diners that have an Atlas Id account on your restaurant Atlas Online website.
</Info>

## Updating a Phone Number on the Restaurant’s Website

Diners can follow these steps to update their phone number:

1. **Log in to the restaurant’s website:**

   ![Notion Image](https://www.notion.so/image/https%3A%2F%2Fprod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2F8aee948b-9843-4907-8b92-c79e8ee8b8fc%2F1d3984af-067a-4a7c-8cc8-ad0255f62f51%2FCleanShot_2024-08-21_at_14.15.072x.png?table=block&id=24cc6c59-ee57-4629-90b1-a600efcddb06&cache=v2)

   * Click on the **user icon** in the top-right corner.

   * Click on **Log In**.

2. **Enter login credentials:**

   * On the **Log In** page, enter the registered email address.

   * Click **Log In**.

3. **Recover access if the phone number is unavailable:**

   ![Notion Image](https://www.notion.so/image/https%3A%2F%2Fprod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2F8aee948b-9843-4907-8b92-c79e8ee8b8fc%2F7f45ccae-24a6-439d-b65d-d9285d42bf44%2FCleanShot_2024-08-21_at_14.17.232x.png?table=block&id=1cee4b68-ef1a-4d9e-b19f-41e6bba07f3a&cache=v2)

   * If access to the registered phone number is lost, select **Send code to email instead**.

   * Enter the access code received via email.

4. **Navigate to the profile page:**

   ![Notion Image](https://www.notion.so/image/https%3A%2F%2Fprod-files-secure.s3.us-west-2.amazonaws.com%2F8aee948b-9843-4907-8b92-c79e8ee8b8fc%2Faa8d536b-8476-4791-bd73-a60573fe60c9%2FCleanShot_2024-08-21_at_14.20.292x.png?table=block&id=7db611fa-9a13-4393-9c0c-68e43c93b5ca&cache=v2)

   * After logging in, the page will redirect to the **landing page**.

   * Click on the **user icon** in the top-right corner.

   * Select **Profile**.

5. **Update the phone number:**

   * Click on the **Mobile Number** field.

   * Enter the new phone number.

   * Click **Update Your Profile**.

6. **Verify the update:**

   * The phone number should now be updated.

   * If any issues persist, diners can email [**hello@atlas.kitchen**]() for further assistance.
