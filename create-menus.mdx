## Adding Products to a Section

1. Open a **section** and tap **Add a Product**.

2. Choose to add a **new** or **existing** product.

3. Fill in the required fields:**Best practice:** Use a **1:1 aspect ratio** for images with blank space around the edges.

   * **Name**

   * **Currency & Price**

   * *Description* (Accepts HTML tags for formatting)

   * *Tags* (Used for printing and KDS configurations)

   * *Brand*

   * *Image* (PNG/JPG, up to 10MB)

## Configuring Modifier Groups

1. Open a **product**, then go to **Configuration**.

2. Tap **Select Modifier Groups**.

3. Choose to add a **new** or **existing** modifier group.

   * **Modifier Groups** allow diners to make choices within a product (e.g., Choice of Doneness).

4. Fill in the required fields:

   * **Name**

   * **Minimum selection** (Set to `0` to make this optional)

   * **Maximum selection** (Set to `1` if only one choice is allowed)

   * *Maximum selection per modifier* (Leave blank if unrestricted)

## Adding Modifiers to a Modifier Group

1. Open a **modifier group** and tap **Select Modifiers**.

2. Choose to add **new** or **existing** modifiers.

   * **Modifiers** are the individual choices within a group (e.g., Hot or Cold).

3. Fill in the optional fields:

   * *Price override* (Overrides the original item price within the modifier group)

   * *Default quantity* (Assigns a pre-selected default quantity, useful for bundles)

Once configured, the menu will be ready for use in **Atlas Online**, **POS**, or **Kiosk**.
